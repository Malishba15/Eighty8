What makes Us Unique

Eighty8 Beauty Salon stands out for its fusion of modern trends with timeless elegance, offering a sanctuary where clients experience personalized beauty transformations. With an emphasis on holistic care, they integrate cutting-edge techniques with natural products, fostering a rejuvenating atmosphere. The salon's talented team not only provides exceptional hair, makeup, and skincare services but also cultivates a community centered on self-expression and confidence. Through bespoke consultations and innovative treatments, Eighty8 crafts bespoke experiences tailored to each client's individual needs, ensuring a truly unforgettable salon journey.

Our Facilities

A spacious and comfortable waiting area where clients can relax before their appointments, complete with refreshments and reading materials.
Individual treatment rooms equipped with modern amenities and privacy features to ensure a relaxing and intimate experience for clients during their services.
A dedicated space for bridal parties to prepare for weddings or special events, featuring hairstyling stations, makeup counters, and ample space for dressing and pre-ceremony activities.
A boutique within the salon offering a curated selection of professional haircare, skincare, and beauty products for clients to purchase and continue their pampering routines at home.
A serene relaxation area equipped with spa amenities such as heated massage chairs, aromatherapy, and soothing music to enhance the overall experience for clients.
A designated area within the salon where parents can bring their children, complete with toys, books, and entertainment options to keep young ones occupied while they receive services.
